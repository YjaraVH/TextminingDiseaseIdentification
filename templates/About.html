About the project

This application is being developed at the request of UMC Utrecht (Wilhelmina Children's Hospital).

This application uses text mining to diagnose metabolic diseases by searching through a database.

This database is build out of variouse tables contaning information about:

  • Metabolites
  • Diseases
  • Types of fluid
  • Patient ID's
  • Z-scores
  • Pathways of the metabolites
  • Tissues
  • Origins
  • Relevance
  • PubMed articles
  • PubMed disease
  • PubMed genes

    • As a result, the condition can be identified and treated more quickly.

    Excel files can be uploaded at the Homepage.
